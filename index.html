About Me

I'm Julie Torres, a 19-year-old from Dallas, TX, specifically Oak Cliff. Currently, I'm juggling the responsibilities of a full-time Merchandising Manager role at Five Below while pursuing my degree. Outside of work and studies, I find joy in playing with my two adorable dogs and indulging in my passion for baking. Currently, I'm dedicated to my studies, pursuing a degree that aligns with my aspirations. An internship with IBM during the summer of 2022 was a significant milestone, and with the completion of my degree on the horizon, I'm eager to return to IBM and continue contributing to the tech world.

Something I don't normally tell people is that I was born on a leap year. Being born on a leap year has always added a unique twist to my life. While I technically celebrate my birthday every four years, turning five this year feels like a special milestone. It's like having a secret code embedded in my birth date, making it stand out among the rest. Explaining the concept of leap years to others has become a fun and recurring conversation topic. People often find it fascinating, and I've come to embrace the quirks that come with being born on such an uncommon date. It adds a touch of rarity to my identity, making each birthday celebration all the more memorable.

Hobbies

I absolutely love spending my free time engaged in various activities that bring me joy and relaxation. One of my favorite pastimes is playing soccer and volleyball, not for any professional aspirations, but purely for the thrill of the game and the camaraderie it brings. There's something about the rush of energy on the field or the sandy court that allows me to unwind and enjoy the physical activity. It's a fantastic way to stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle while having a blast with friends.

When I'm not running around on the sports field, you can find me in the kitchen, baking up a storm. I've developed a genuine passion for creating delicious treats, from cookies and cakes to pastries. Baking is not just a hobby for me; it's a form of creative expression where I can experiment with flavors and techniques. The satisfaction of sharing these homemade delights with my family and friends is incomparable. Additionally, I'm not one to shy away from a friendly video game session, especially when it involves some quality bonding time with my two younger brothers. The combination of sports, baking, and gaming truly reflects the diverse and enjoyable aspects of my life outside of work and academic pursuits.
